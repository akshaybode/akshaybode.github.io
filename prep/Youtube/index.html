🧭 The Big Picture

Every business (Amazon, Netflix, Zomato) wants to understand customers, increase profit, and improve processes. The most reliable way is to base decisions on data instead of assumptions. Data Engineering is the discipline that moves and shapes that data so Data Scientists, Analysts, and ML Engineers can extract value.

The golden rule of the whole course: Everything you build must create value for the business. The fanciest algorithm or tool is useless if it doesn't help cut costs, improve a process, or understand the customer.

Where Data Engineering Sits

Data Engineering is the "plumber" between data generation and data consumption — it takes data from a source, transforms it, and delivers it to the next stage.
