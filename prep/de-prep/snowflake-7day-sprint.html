7-Day Snowflake Sprint + SnowPro Core

For a quick learner who already knows SQL. ~5–6 hrs/day. Goal: functional Snowflake + a project by Day 5, cert-ready by Day 7.

The efficiency unlock: the project is your cert prep. Loading (stages, COPY INTO, Snowpipe), warehouses, micro-partitions, clustering, caching, semi-structured, Time Travel & cloning are both SnowPro Core topics and project tasks. Don't study them twice — do the project to practice for the exam.

Don't sit the exam below 80% on practice. A fail costs the retake fee and time. If you're not there by Day 7, push the exam 2–3 days — the learning is done, you just need the practice scores to confirm it.

Critical success factors

  1. Practice exams are non-negotiable. The course gets you ~60%; the practice tests + reviewing wrong answers get you to a pass.
  2. Hands-on > passive watching. Play the course at 1.5–2×, then do it in the console. Aim ~2 hrs of clicking per 1 hr of video.
  3. Time-box the project. A working load → star schema is enough this week. Don't gold-plate it.
  4. Apply by Day 5. Don't let the cert delay job applications — functional Snowflake + a project is already interview-ready. The cert is the cherry on top.

Exam logistics

SnowPro Core (COF-C02) — ~100 questions, ~115 min, pass ~750/1000, ~$175 USD, online proctored. Verify current details on the official Snowflake certification page before booking.
