About Me

From application engineering to building the data backbone behind telecom analytics.

I'm a Data Engineer who loves the unglamorous-but-critical work of making data trustworthy, fast, and available. Over 5+ years at Amdocs and IBM I've built batch and streaming pipelines that process 10M+ telecom billing, ordering, and subscriber records every day.
